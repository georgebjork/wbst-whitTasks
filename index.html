What can it do?

#1
Organization

WhitTask will provide clear and easy organization of a students day through the "My Day" page. This page will show students assingments due that day.

#2
Blackboard

WhitTasks will have connectivty to the users Blackboard account. This will allow WhitTasks be able to pull in new assingments and show due dates.

#3
Whitworth Activites

WhitTasks will show students sporting events, actives and speakers on their "My Day" page. Any upcoming events can be found in the "Campus Life" page.

#4
Reminders

WhitTaks will be able to provide students with clear and on time reminders of assingments and campus actives.

#5
UI

WhitTasks provides students a clean, modern, and intuitive user interface that is easy to navigate and understand.

#6
Campus Alerts

WhitTasks will alert students of current campus anouncments and updates rather than having to recive an email.
